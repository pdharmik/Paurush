Get Help

Need Help? On this page you will find a series of training tutorials that will guide you through the basic functions of the Lexmark Global Services Portal. Each tutorial will cover a different Services Portal page and walk you through the functionality available on that page. Access to tutorials is determined by your individual business needs and assigned role. Once you have viewed an entire video, you may go back at a later date to reference particular sections of the video through the table of contents, accessible in the bottom right corner of each video. This will allow you to skip to the section of the video that you need to view.

 

Site Navigation and Homepage Features
This tutorial provides you with the knowledge necessary to operate the basic functions of the site. The topics covered in this tutorial include site navigation, toolbar functionality, and homepage features.

 

Device Finder
This tutorial walks you through the process of locating a device and viewing the information related to that device.

 

Page Counts
This tutorial demonstrates how to update page counts online for a select group of devices or upload an Excel spreadsheet with updates for a large batch of devices. This tutorial also provides instructions on how to verify that a page count update is submitted successfully.

Service Requests
This tutorial provides instructions about how to locate a Service Request and view Service Request details.

 

Reports
This tutorial demonstrates how to run, schedule, and view a report.

 

Document Library
This tutorial walks you through the process of locating and viewing documents such as user guides, training materials, and white papers.
